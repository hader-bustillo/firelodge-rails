Our Mission

Your best holiday yet! No doubt about it.

We are parents to young kids who love playing at this mountain retreat. As avid travelers and long-time Airbnb/Vrbo renters ourselves, we wanted to create a special experience for families looking for a carefree, magical vacation. Our goal is to make you feel safe, supported, and inspired.

The tight-knit community here in Snoqualmie Pass is also very close to our hearts, so we find various ways to support it through Fire Lodge. You’ll find things like local art hanging on the walls, and gift cards to nearby businesses.

When we’re not hosting at our stunning property, you can find us snowmobiling around Snoqualmie Pass, dirt biking in Cle Elum, or building epic Lego creations with our kids.
